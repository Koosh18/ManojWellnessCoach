Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers to the most common questions clients ask before starting their journey.

How long does weight loss usually take? ›

It depends on your starting point, health condition, and how consistently you follow the plan. On average, clients start noticing visible changes in 4–6 weeks, with deeper transformation over 3–6 months. During your first discussion, Manoj will give you a more personalised expectation.

Are the herbal products safe? ›

Yes. The herbal products recommended are chosen carefully for safety and quality. They are designed to support metabolism, digestion, and energy in a natural way. If you have any medical condition or are on medication, it will be discussed during your consultation before finalising your plan.

Do I need to exercise? ›

Movement is helpful but you do not need heavy gym workouts to start. Depending on your current fitness level, Manoj may recommend light walking, stretching, or simple home exercises that fit into your schedule. The core results come from nutrition, herbal support, and lifestyle changes.

Is online coaching available? ›

Yes, online coaching is fully available via phone, video calls, and WhatsApp. You do not need to be in the same city to work with Manoj. All progress tracking, plan adjustments, and support can be done remotely.
